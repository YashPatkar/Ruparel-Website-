All About Ruparel

Ruparel College is a prestigious educational institution located in Matunga, Mumbai, India. The college was established in 1964 and is affiliated with the University of Mumbai. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields such as Arts, Science, and Commerce.

The campus of Ruparel College is spread over an area of 2 acres and has state-of-the-art facilities such as well-equipped laboratories, computer labs, library, sports facilities, and auditorium. The college also has a dedicated placement cell that provides guidance and assistance to students in finding employment opportunities after graduation.
